About

MapLink is a Website and mobile application device that is used for helping people to get a up-to-date news. This application will provide you information about breaking news around the globe.

This application is for people who have a mobile device or tablet. So they can simply just click on which country they prefer to watch the breaking news, then it will show you the link and the video. Additionally, right now you can only watch the breaking news in some countries. In the future, this application will be developed in to a more sophisticated application and provide news all over the world.
